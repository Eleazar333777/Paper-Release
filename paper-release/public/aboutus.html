Discover the passionate researchers, students, and innovators driving the future of water treatment technology and PFAS remediation through cutting-edge laboratory research and data science.

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between academic research and real-world water treatment decisions by centralizing PFAS filtration data into one accessible, research-grade platform. Our database serves as a hub for high-quality, peer-reviewed studies, facilitating global collaboration among researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers.

As water contamination challenges evolve, we remain committed to continuously expanding our database with the latest scientific findings and technological developments. Our goal is to empower our community with timely and relevant information, enabling informed decisions and fostering sustainable solutions for clean water worldwide.
